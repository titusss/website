Developing an Pixel-to-CNC algorithm & implementation for painting AI generated art with an industrial robot.

Abstract

With the exponential growth of computing power in modern computer systems, AI development has made significant progress in recent years. Today’s algorithms are able to generate art whose aesthetics are sometimes hardly distinguishable from human ones. Meanwhile, automation in the physical world starts to evolve into a reactive and fully autonomous process. This project‘s goal is to let an industrial robot paint pictures which are completly generated by a Machine Learning algorithm.
